Salt Water Murris is a not-for-profit organisation predominately comprised of descendants of the original Nunukal, Nughie and Goenpul tribes of the region.

The indigenous people of Minjerribah within the Quandamooka region have an uninterrupted history of living on the island that dates back thousands of years.

Our people tell stories and pass on cultural knowledge through our art.

Through our art, all the ancestors that lived on Quandamooka from the beginning of time, and through our bloodlines, still exist, as long as we have the art.
